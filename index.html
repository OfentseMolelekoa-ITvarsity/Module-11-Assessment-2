Middle Eastern Fattoush Salad

Beirut, Lebanon

Fattoush is a vibrant, tangy Middle Eastern salad featuring a colorful mix of crisp vegetables like cucumbers, tomatoes, and radishes, all tossed with fresh herbs and a zesty lemon and olive oil dressing. The star of this dish is the crunchy, toasted pieces of pita bread that add a delightful texture to every bite. With its bright flavors and refreshing ingredients, this Fattoush Salad is a perfect balance of healthy, light, and satisfying

MFresh Mediterranean Salad with Toasted Pita

Damascus, Syria

Fresh and vibrant, the MFresh Mediterranean Salad combines crisp vegetables, tangy feta cheese, and Kalamata olives, all tossed in a light, zesty dressing. Paired with perfectly toasted pita wedges for that satisfying crunch, this salad offers a healthy and flavorful Mediterranean experience. Ideal for a light lunch or a side dish, it's a delightful blend of textures and flavors that will leave you refreshed and satisfied.

Raspberry Pie

Seattle, USA

A delectable treat that blends the sweet-tart flavors of fresh raspberries with a buttery, flaky crust. This pie is perfect for any occasion, whether it's a cozy family gathering or a special celebration. The natural tanginess of the raspberries is balanced by the richness of the filling, offering a satisfying dessert experience. Topped with a dusting of powdered sugar or a scoop of vanilla ice cream, this raspberry pie is a crowd-pleaser that combines the essence of summer in every bite.
